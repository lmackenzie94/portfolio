About Me

Simply put, I am a hard-working young man with big dreams trying to make my mother proud.

Upon completing my BBA degree at Laurier in 2016, I landed a great job at Chubb Insurance as a Business Analyst. For two years, I worked with an incredible group of people doing interesting and challenging work. Somewhere along the way I decided to teach myself HTML out of interest and before I knew it I was taking part-time web development courses and found myself considering a complete career change.

On January 4th, 2019 I quit my job.

I've since completed HackerYou's nine-week Web Development Bootcamp and I'm incredibly excited to be on the hunt for my first developer job.

During my free time, you'll find me with a golf club in my hand or a 6-string around my neck.
